# User Guide — ASCII Camera

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
2. Click **Start Camera** at the bottom of the left sidebar
3. Allow camera permission when prompted
4. Your live ASCII art appears in the main preview area

The image is mirrored like a selfie camera. An FPS counter shows in the top-right corner.

---

## Sidebar Tabs

The left sidebar has 3 tabs:

### Controls Tab
All image adjustment sliders and ASCII configuration:
- **Brightness** (-100 to +100) — Lighten or darken
- **Contrast** (-100 to +100) — Sharper or flatter
- **Exposure** (0.5x to 2.0x) — Simulate over/under exposure
- **Saturation** (0% to 200%) — Color intensity
- **Sharpness** (0 to 100) — Edge enhancement
- **Gamma** (0.2 to 3.0) — Tonal curve
- **Resolution** (3 to 16) — Lower = more detail (more characters)
- **Charset** dropdown — Choose ASCII character set

### Filters Tab
Click any filter pill to apply instantly:
- None, B&W, Retro, Invert, Neon, Blueprint, Thermal, Posterize

Also includes **Background Color** presets (Dark, Darker, Midnight, Light).

### Export Tab
Configure how your photo is saved:
- **Format** — PNG, JPG, or TXT
- **Image Scale** (1x to 5x) — Higher = larger image file
- **Font Color** — Auto (matches filter) or pick a specific color

---

## Taking & Saving a Photo

1. Adjust filters and sliders to get the look you want
2. Click **Capture** — the current ASCII frame is frozen
3. The captured frame appears in a panel at the bottom (click to toggle)
4. Click **Download** — saves in your selected format (PNG/JPG/TXT)
5. Click **Copy** — copies ASCII text to clipboard

---

## Tips

- **Good lighting** gives more detail in the ASCII output
- **Resolution 3–5** produces very detailed ASCII art (may be slower)
- Use **Detailed charset** for the best gradient quality in captured photos
- **Blocks charset** creates a pixel mosaic effect
- **Neon filter + dark background** looks great for sharing
- For social media sharing, use **PNG** export at **3x–5x scale**
- Works offline once loaded
- Works on mobile — layout adapts to small screens

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Camera not starting | Click "Allow" on browser permission popup |
| Black screen | Check another app isn't using your camera |
| Slow/laggy | Increase Resolution slider (higher = faster) |
| Copy fails | Use HTTPS or localhost for clipboard access |
| Blurry export | Increase image scale in Export tab |
| Font looks wrong | JetBrains Mono loads from Google Fonts (needs internet on first load) |
